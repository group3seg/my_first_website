DISCOVER HOW WE HELP

UNISON IS AN ONLINE CROSS-COMPATIBLE PLATFORM THAT OFFERS MUSICIANS (PRODUCERS, SOUND ENGINEERS, SONGWRITERS, VOCALISTS, ETC.) A CLOUD-BASED COLLABORATIVE ENVIRONMENT.

It allows musicians to publicly or privately share their DAW-based projects (digital audio workstation) on the cloud, allowing anyone to edit their own version.

SEE MORE

Features:

  • Helps musicians working together on different local platforms.
  • Join their work into one single project on the cloud.
  • Converts local DAW files to a cross-compatible intermediary project using AI technology without loss of information.
  • Intermediary projects can be modified and improved by others on any DAW.
  • Users can download intermediary projects from the cloud, modify the project with their prefered DAW and update the intermediary project on the cloud with their work.
  • Users can work on different versions of the same project and combine them into one final project.
